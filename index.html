Who are we?

We are Stream, a digital movement redefining how Africans experience and benefit from entertainment. Built on the belief that your time online should pay you back, Stream connects people, creativity, and opportunity in one continuous flow.

Through partnerships with artists, record labels, movie studios, and content distributors, Stream serves as a bridge between creators and the audience. We help them reach real streamers and in return, they share a portion of their royalties and ad revenue with us. These rewards are then distributed to our users ( the Streamers ) for every collaboration they contribute with these companies, artists and creators.

Stream transforms ordinary digital engagement into real value. Every time you collaborate, you’re not just partnering, you’re participating in a new digital economy where creativity and value flow together.
